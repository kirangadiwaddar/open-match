OpenMatch subscriptions like OpenMatch Plus, Gold, and Platinum “auto-renew”. This means that you will automatically be charged on a recurring basis, depending on the type of subscription you purchased. For example, if you purchase a 6-month OpenMatch Platinum subscription, your subscription will automatically renew at the end of that 6-month period. You must cancel your subscription prior to your auto-renew date to avoid additional charges. Visit this article for steps on how to cancel your subscription. Cancellation steps will vary based on your device, so make sure you’re following the steps that apply to you. Deleting your OpenMatch account will not cancel your subscription renewal.

Whenever the app doesn't immediately recognize your in-app purchases, you can restore your purchase to maintain access to subscriptions and other premium features.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless you cancel your subscription. Deleting the OpenMatch app or your OpenMatch account will not cancel your subscription. Follow the steps below to cancel your OpenMatch subscription.

Does your order number start with MK? This means you made your purchase on the OpenMatch app for iOS and you're billed by Apple.

To cancel your subscription using your iPhone:

  1. Open your iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your name, then tap Subscriptions
  3. Select the subscription you want to cancel
  4. Tap Cancel Subscription

Does your order number start with GPA? This means you made your purchase on the OpenMatch app for Android and you're billed by the Google Play Store.

To cancel your subscription using your Android phone:

  1. Open the Google Play app
  2. At the top right, tap the profile icon
  3. Tap Payments & subscriptions, and then Subscriptions
  4. Select the subscription you want to cancel
  5. Tap Cancel subscription
